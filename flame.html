🧪 Theory: What is a Flame Test?

A flame test is a technique used in chemistry to identify the presence of certain metal ions in a salt based on the characteristic color the metal emits when heated in a flame. The heat excites electrons in the metal, which then emit light of specific wavelengths as they return to their ground state. This gives each element a unique flame color.
