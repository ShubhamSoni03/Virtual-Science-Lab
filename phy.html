✨ About the Virtual Physics Lab

Virtual Physics Lab is a digital platform specially created for Class 11th and 12th students to explore core concepts interactively and visually.

Learn the fascinating science behind projectile motion, electric circuits, and lens systems through real-time simulations.

Adjust values like angles, voltages, and focal lengths to see real-time outcomes — an engaging way to understand physical laws through trial and error.

The intuitive UI and colorful design make your physics journey not just educational but fun, letting you explore at your own pace and curiosity.
