📚 Learn Ohm’s Law

Ohm’s Law is a key concept in electronics. It tells us how voltage (V), resistance (R), and current (I) relate: I = V / R

  • Voltage (V): The push driving electrons
  • Resistance (R): How much the circuit resists flow
  • Current (I): The amount of flow happening

Use the sliders on the right to experiment with different values and watch how current changes in real-time.

🔋 Real-Life Example: Charging a Phone

Imagine your charger gives 5V and your phone circuit has 2.5Ω resistance. According to Ohm’s Law: I = 5 / 2.5 = 2A

So your phone draws 2 Amperes of current while charging!
